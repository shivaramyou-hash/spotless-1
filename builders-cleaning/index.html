Professional Builders Cleaning Services
Throughout Mauritius

Sawdust fills the air, construction materials clutter the rooms, and the remnants of the building process feel overwhelming. That’s where our professional builders cleaning service comes in breathing new life into your home or commercial space after construction or renovation.

Our Winning Builders Cleaning Service

At Spotless, no cleaning challenge is too big or too small. Our reliable builders cleaning teams are trained and equipped with specialised equipment (on request) to take the stress out of cleaning after any building project, whether residential or commercial.

Deep Cleaning of Newly Built or Renovated Spaces

Construction leaves behind more than just a finished space fine dust, debris, paint residue, and hidden particles settle everywhere. Our deep builders cleaning removes these thoroughly, restoring freshness, hygiene, and comfort.

Professional & Trained Staff

Our cleaning professionals undergo rigorous training and are experienced in handling post-construction cleaning for both commercial and residential properties. Every surface is treated with care and precision.

Builders Clean – From Chaos to Completion

Paint marks, varnish, cement, grout, glue, silicon, stickers, and plastic protection can be stubborn after construction. Our team meticulously removes these from tiles, windows, toilets, baths, basins, and surfaces throughout your property.

We clean tile surfaces, floor skirtings, doors, door frames, cupboards (inside and out), lights, switches, windows, and window sills leaving every corner spotless and ready to enjoy.

"Construction chaos, meet your match — Spotless builders cleaning is here to conquer the mess."
Our Worry-Free Promise

You can relax knowing your home or business is in good hands with the Spotless cleaning team. Our friendly, reliable professionals deliver consistently high-quality results. If you’re ever not satisfied with our most recent visit, simply let us know and we will make it right.
