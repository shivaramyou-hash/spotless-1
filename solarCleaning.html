Solar Cleaning Process

02
01

Site Inspection

Assessing solar panels for dust buildup, stains, and overall condition before starting the cleaning process.

02

Safety Preparation

Securing the area and using safety equipment to ensure safe access to rooftop or elevated solar installations.

03

Dry Debris Removal

Removing loose dust, leaves, and bird droppings using soft brushes and air blowers.

04

Water-Based Cleaning

Washing panels with purified water and non-abrasive tools to avoid scratches and mineral residue.

05

Stain & Spot Treatment

Treating stubborn stains and bird droppings with eco-friendly solutions safe for solar glass.

06

Performance Check

Inspecting panels for cleanliness and ensuring maximum sunlight absorption after cleaning.

07

Final Review & Client Approval

Reviewing results with the client and providing maintenance recommendations for optimal performance.
