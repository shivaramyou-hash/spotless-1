Professional Commercial & Industrial Cleaning
Services Throughout Mauritius

The warehouse is dirty, the commercial property need a clean up, the offices are in disarray, and the office bins are not being emptied. But that’s where our professional commercial and industrial cleaning services come in to transform your business back to its sparkling condition.

Our Winning Commercial & Industrial Cleaning Process

Knowing the exact cleaning requirements of each of our customer’s businesses, offices or buildings is crucial for a successful and long-term relationship. At Spotless, we use a specific method to ensure that each client’s needs are met.

Assessment, Personal Touch & Reliability

Our process begins with an on-site assessment to understand your specific cleaning requirements. We then provide a tailored quote and service plan. While we operate with professional procedures, equipment, and chemicals, we pride ourselves on delivering a personal touch through our dedicated team.

Contract, Placement & Once-Off Cleaning Solutions

We offer flexible commercial and industrial cleaning solutions, including contract cleaning, placement cleaning, and once-off deep cleans. Our detailed checklists ensure your facility is cleaned top to bottom, whether services are scheduled weekly, bi-weekly, bi-monthly, or monthly.

"A clean and organized work environment is the foundation of productivity, safety, and professional excellence."

From warehouses and offices to commercial buildings and industrial facilities, Spotless delivers reliable, supervised cleaning services using trained staff, quality equipment, and proven systems. With our Worry-Free Promise, you can relax knowing your business is in safe hands and if you’re ever not satisfied, we’ll make it right.
